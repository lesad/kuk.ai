# peep-rs

## Changelog

Keep `CHANGELOG.md` up to date on every meaningful change:

- Add entries under `[Unreleased]` as work progresses
- Follow [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/) format: `Added`, `Changed`, `Deprecated`, `Removed`, `Fixed`, `Security`
- On release: rename `[Unreleased]` to `[x.y.z] - YYYY-MM-DD` and bump `version` in `Cargo.toml`
